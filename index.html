Claim to relevance

Making sense of all the information available today can be difficult, especially with only a feeble human body to do so. That is why we exist to aid you, fellow pursuers of human wellbeing. If any of the four pursuits listed underneath are of concern, then this website is relevant for you.

Survival

Whether on a personal or societal scale, survival is the concern of many. Without it, most goals are unachievable. New threats are arising today to human survival, bringing with them new solutions.
